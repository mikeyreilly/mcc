How to tackle introduction of DW_TAG_lexical_blocks

SemanticAnalysis is implicitly creating scopes but the rest of the
compiler never sees where these scopes start or end. It would be
straight-forward to associate each variable with the correct scope
object in SemanticAnalysis but how can we have low and
high pc values for the scope? We could introduce a new BlockItem: Scope(id, startOrEnd)
